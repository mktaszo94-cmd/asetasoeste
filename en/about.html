About the West Zone Darts Association

The West Zone Darts Association was founded from a shared passion for the sport of darts and the desire to bring together players, teams, and enthusiasts from the Oeste region into a strong, inclusive, and growing project.

More than just a sports association, we are a community that promotes social interaction, sportsmanship, and personal development through the practice of darts, valuing both competitive play and recreational enjoyment. We work daily to create opportunities, organize events, and strengthen the sport in our region, always guided by the principles of fair play, mutual respect, and friendship.

We believe that sport has the power to bring people together, build connections, and contribute to a more active, healthy, and engaged society.

Mission

To promote, develop, and grow the practice of darts in the Oeste region by encouraging participation from players of all ages and skill levels, supporting local clubs and organizations, and fostering a healthy, competitive, and inclusive environment for all participants.

Vision

To become a reference association at both regional and national levels, recognized for the quality of its organization, the sustainable growth of the sport, and its ability to unite people around darts, contributing to its continuous development and recognition.

Values

  • Sportsmanship - We promote fair play, respect, and ethical behavior in all competitions and activities.
  • Community - We believe in teamwork and in strengthening relationships between players, teams, and partners.
  • Inclusion - Darts is for everyone. We value diversity, equality, and barrier-free participation.
  • Commitment - We act with responsibility, transparency, and dedication in everything we do.
  • Development - We invest in the continuous growth of players, the association, and the sport.
